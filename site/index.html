HNReader

A fast, private Hacker News reader.

Browse the front page, read articles in a clean reader mode, and follow comment threads — no ads, no tracking, no account.

Download on the App Store

Reader mode

Strips away clutter so you can read the article, not the page chrome.

Proper comment ordering

Threads are shown in the same order as Hacker News, with collapsible replies.

Private by design

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your preferences stay on your device.

Yours to tune

Themes, fonts, text size, and an orange you can actually pick.

Block authors

Mute any author with a tap — their stories disappear and their comments read “[blocked].”

Fast & light

Built as a lean native app with offline caching of recent stories.
